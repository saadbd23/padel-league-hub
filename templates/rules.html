1 League Structure & Format

Swiss Format: The league uses a Swiss tournament format where teams are paired based on their current standings and performance.

Round Structure:

  • Each round lasts one week (Monday to Sunday)
  • New rounds generated every Monday morning
  • Round 1 starts on Monday, November 17, 2025
  • One match per round against different opponents

Pairing System:

  • Teams paired by similar records (wins/losses)
  • No repeat opponents in league stage
  • Byes given if odd number of teams
2 Team & Free Agent Registration

Team Registration:

  • Register as a complete team (2 players)
  • Provide team name, both players' info
  • Player 2 must confirm via SMS/WhatsApp link
  • Admin reviews and confirms registration

Free Agent Registration:

Don't have a partner? Register as a Free Agent and we'll match you with another solo player.

  • Skill Levels: Beginner, Intermediate, Advanced

  • Matched pairs auto-convert to teams

Skill Level Honesty:

  • Beginner: New or <6 months experience
  • Intermediate: 6mo-2yrs, comfortable with basics
  • Advanced: 2+ years, strong technical skills

  • Sunday 23:59: Match deadline

Booking Process:

  • Team A proposes date/time
  • Team B confirms or suggests different time
  • Coordinate via WhatsApp/phone first
  • Can schedule any day Monday-Sunday

4 Match Format & Scoring

Match Format:

  • Best of 3 sets
  • Standard padel scoring (games to 6, tie-break at 6-6)
  • Win by 2 sets (e.g., 6-4, 6-3 or 6-4, 3-6, 6-2)

Court Booking:

  • Typical booking: 90 minutes
  • May extend if court available
  • Arrive 10 minutes early for warm-up

Equipment:

  • Bring your own paddle
  • Balls provided by venue
  • Appropriate court shoes required
5 Reschedule Policy

Allowance:

  • 2 reschedules per team for entire league
  • No reschedules in playoffs
  • Tracked per team, not per match

üìÖ Request by Wednesday 23:59

After Wednesday, requests not accepted for that round

Reschedule Window:

  • Only Monday-Wednesday of following week
  • Thursday-Sunday not available
  • Date picker shows valid dates only

‚ö†Ô∏è Makeup Deadline: Next Wed 23:59

If not completed, automatic walkover to opponent

Double Match Week:

  • Will play 2 matches in one week
  • Makeup match + regular match
  • Both count towards standings
6 Substitute Player Policy

Allowance:

  • 2 substitutes during league stage
  • No subs in playoffs
  • Tracked per team

Requirements:

  • Provide sub's name, phone, email
  • Admin approval required before match
  • Submit in advance (not day of match)

Email Notifications:

  • Request: All 3 parties notified (P1, P2, Sub)
  • Approval: All 3 receive confirmation
  • Denial: All 3 receive notice

Restrictions:

  • Can't be registered players on other teams
  • Same sub can be used multiple times
  • Both members can't be subbed same match
7 Score Submission & Confirmation

Submission Process:

  • Winning team submits score first
  • Enter from your team's perspective
  • Format: Set 1, Set 2, Set 3 (if played)
  • Example: Won 6-4, 6-3, enter exactly that

Confirmation:

  • Opponent notified to confirm score
  • They see result with winner and score
  • One-click to approve
  • Can click "Dispute" if incorrect

Disputed Scores:

  • Admin notified immediately
  • Admin contacts both teams to resolve
  • Admin enters correct score after verification
  • Match stays "pending" until resolved

Stats Update:

  • Once confirmed, stats auto-update
  • Team standings: wins, losses, sets, games, points
  • Individual player stats updated
  • Leaderboards update in real-time
8 Standings & Rankings

Ranking Criteria (in order):

  1. Points: Win=3, Draw=1, Loss=0
  2. Head-to-Head: If tied on points
  3. Set Difference: Sets won - sets lost
  4. Game Difference: Games won - games lost
  5. Sets Won: Total sets won
  6. Games Won: Total games won

Leaderboards:

  • Team Leaderboard: Overall rankings
  • Player Leaderboard: Individual stats
  • Auto-update after each confirmed match
  • Separate stats tabs for detailed analysis

Playoff Qualification:

  • Top teams qualify (number based on total teams)
  • Format announced after league completion
  • Tiebreakers follow criteria above
9 Walkovers & No-Shows

Automatic walkovers for:

  • Regular match not done by Sunday 23:59
  • Makeup match not done by Wednesday 23:59

Walkover Score:

  • Default: 6-0, 6-0 to ready team
  • Full 3 points to walkover winner
  • Stats auto-update (2 sets, 12 games)

Admin Override:

  • Can reverse in emergency situations
  • Must be genuine emergency only
  • Contact admin immediately if emergency
10 Fair Play & Conduct

Expected Behavior:

  • Demonstrate good sportsmanship
  • Respect opponents and officials
  • Call lines honestly and fairly
  • Be punctual for scheduled matches

Prohibited Conduct:

  • Unsportsmanlike behavior
  • Verbal abuse or intimidation
  • Intentional cheating
  • Equipment abuse

Violations:

  • Warning for first offense
  • Point penalty for repeated offense
  • Match forfeit for serious violations
  • League suspension for extreme cases
11 Disputes & Resolution

Resolution Process:

  • Try to resolve amicably between teams first
  • If unresolved, contact league admin
  • Admin reviews evidence from both sides
  • Admin makes final decision

Types of Disputes:

  • Score disagreements
  • Match scheduling conflicts
  • Conduct violations
  • Rule interpretation questions

Evidence Needed:

  • Screenshots of communications
  • Witness statements if available
  • Match photos/videos if relevant
  • Clear description of the issue
