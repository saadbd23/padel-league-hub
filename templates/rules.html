1 League Structure & Format

Swiss Format: The league uses a Swiss tournament format where teams are paired based on their current standings and performance.

Round Structure:

  • Each round lasts one week (Monday to Sunday)
  • New rounds are generated every Monday morning by the admin
  • Round 1 starts on Monday, January 6, 2025
  • Teams play one match per round against different opponents

Pairing System:

  • Teams are paired based on similar records (wins/losses)
  • No team plays the same opponent twice in league stage
  • Byes may be given if there's an odd number of teams

2 Team & Free Agent Registration

Team Registration:

  • Register as a complete team (2 players)
  • Provide team name, both players' names, phone numbers, and emails
  • Player 2 must confirm participation via SMS/WhatsApp link
  • Admin reviews and confirms team registration

Free Agent Registration:

‚≠ê Solo Players Welcome!

Don't have a partner? Register as a Free Agent and we'll match you with another solo player.

  • Register individually with your name, phone, email, and skill level
  • Skill Level Selection: Beginner, Intermediate, or Advanced
  • Admin will pair you with another free agent of similar skill level
  • Matched pairs are automatically converted into teams

Skill Level Honesty:

üéØ Be Honest About Your Skill Level

Admin will make best efforts to match skill levels fairly. Accurate self-assessment ensures better matches and more enjoyable games for everyone. If you overstate your level, you and your partner may struggle unnecessarily. If you understate it, matches may be too easy.

  • Beginner: New to padel or less than 6 months experience
  • Intermediate: 6 months to 2 years, comfortable with basic shots and strategy
  • Advanced: 2+ years, compete regularly, strong technical skills

Pairing Process:

  • Free agents are listed in the admin panel
  • Admin pairs players based on skill level and availability
  • Both players are notified when paired
  • New team must confirm and choose a team name
  • Once confirmed, team enters the league like any other team

3 Match Scheduling & Deadlines

Weekly Timeline:

  • Monday 9:00 AM: New round generated by admin
  • Monday-Sunday: Teams coordinate and play their match
  • Sunday 23:59: Match deadline (must be completed and score submitted)

Booking Matches:

  • Team A proposes a date/time for the match
  • Team B can confirm with one click or suggest a different time
  • Both teams should coordinate via WhatsApp/phone before proposing
  • Matches can be scheduled any day Monday-Sunday

Important Deadlines:

‚ö†Ô∏è Regular Matches: Sunday 23:59

If not completed by Sunday 23:59, automatic walkover will be awarded

4 Match Format & Scoring

Match Format:

  • Best of 3 sets
  • Standard padel scoring (games to 6, tie-break at 6-6)
  • Win by 2 sets (e.g., 6-4, 6-3 or 6-4, 3-6, 6-2)

Court Booking:

  • Typical booking: 90 minutes
  • Matches may extend if court availability permits
  • Teams should arrive 10 minutes early for warm-up

Equipment:

  • Each player must bring their own paddle
  • Balls are typically provided by the venue
  • Appropriate court shoes required

5 Reschedule Policy

Reschedule Allowance:

  • Each team gets 2 reschedules for the entire league stage
  • No reschedules allowed in playoffs
  • Reschedules are tracked per team, not per match

Request Deadline:

üìÖ Must request by Wednesday 23:59 of the current round

After Wednesday, reschedule requests are not accepted for that round

Reschedule Window:

  • Can only reschedule to Monday-Wednesday of the following week
  • Thursday-Sunday dates are not available for reschedules
  • Date picker will only show valid dates

Makeup Match Deadline:

‚ö†Ô∏è Wednesday 23:59 of the following week

If makeup match is not completed by this deadline, automatic walkover is awarded to opponent

Two Matches in One Week:

  • Teams with rescheduled matches will play 2 matches in one week
  • One makeup match (from previous round) + one regular match (current round)
  • Both matches count towards standings

Admin Review:

  • All reschedule requests require admin approval
  • Admin will notify opponent team of the reschedule
  • Emergency situations can be discussed with admin

6 Substitute Player Policy

Substitute Allowance:

  • Each team may use 2 substitutes during the league stage
  • No substitutes allowed in playoffs
  • Substitutes are tracked per team

Substitute Requirements:

  • Must provide substitute's full name, phone, and email
  • Substitute must be approved by admin before the match
  • Request must be submitted in advance (not day of match)

Email Notifications:

  • When request submitted: All 3 parties receive notification (Player 1, Player 2, Substitute)
  • When admin approves: All 3 parties receive confirmation
  • When admin denies: All 3 parties receive denial notice

Substitute Restrictions:

  • Substitutes cannot be registered players on other teams
  • Same substitute can be used multiple times (counts as 1 sub per use)
  • Both team members cannot be substituted in the same match

7 Score Submission & Confirmation

Score Submission Process:

  • After match completion, winning team submits the score first
  • Score must be entered from your team's perspective
  • Format: Set 1, Set 2, Set 3 (if played)
  • Example: If you won 6-4, 6-3, enter exactly that

Score Confirmation:

  • Opponent team receives notification to confirm score
  • They see the result with winner name and score
  • One-click confirmation to approve
  • If score is incorrect, they can click "Dispute"

Disputed Scores:

  • Admin is notified immediately
  • Admin will contact both teams to resolve
  • Admin enters the correct score after verification
  • Match remains "pending" until resolved

Statistics Update:

  • Once both teams confirm, stats are automatically updated
  • Team standings: wins, losses, sets, games, points
  • Individual player stats: matches played, wins, losses
  • Leaderboards update in real-time

8 Standings & Rankings

Ranking Criteria (in order):

  1. Points: Win = 3 points, Draw = 1 point, Loss = 0 points
  2. Head-to-Head: If tied on points, head-to-head result decides
  3. Set Difference: Sets won minus sets lost
  4. Game Difference: Games won minus games lost
  5. Sets Won: Total sets won
  6. Games Won: Total games won

Leaderboards:

  • Team Leaderboard: Overall team rankings
  • Player Leaderboard: Individual player statistics
  • Both update automatically after each confirmed match
  • Separate stats tabs available for detailed analysis

Playoff Qualification:

  • Top teams qualify for playoffs (exact number determined by total teams)
  • Playoff format announced after league stage completion
  • Tiebreakers strictly follow the ranking criteria above

9 Walkovers & No-Shows

Automatic Walkovers:

System automatically awards walkovers for:

  • Regular match not completed by Sunday 23:59
  • Makeup match not completed by Wednesday 23:59

Walkover Score:

  • Default score: 6-0, 6-0 to the team who was ready to play
  • Full 3 points awarded to walkover winner
  • Stats updated automatically (2 sets, 12 games for winner)

Admin Override:

  • Admin can manually reverse walkovers in emergency situations
  • Must be genuine emergency (hospitalization, family emergency, etc.)
  • Contact admin immediately if emergency occurs

No-Show Policy:

  • If a team doesn't show up for a scheduled match: Walkover to opponent
  • Repeated no-shows may result in team disqualification
  • Always communicate with opponent if unable to make scheduled time

10 Fair Play & Conduct

Expected Behavior:

  • Demonstrate good sportsmanship at all times
  • Respect opponents, venue staff, and other players
  • Make fair line calls; give benefit of doubt to opponent
  • Keep matches moving at a reasonable pace

Communication:

  • Respond to opponent's booking requests within 24 hours
  • Notify opponent immediately if you need to reschedule
  • Be punctual for scheduled matches
  • Use WhatsApp group or direct contact for coordination

Prohibited Conduct:

  • Unsportsmanlike behavior or verbal abuse
  • Intentional gamesmanship or time-wasting
  • Making consistently bad line calls
  • Damaging venue property or equipment

Penalties:

  • First offense: Warning from admin
  • Second offense: Point deduction or match forfeit
  • Severe offense: Team disqualification from league

11 Disputes & Resolution

Types of Disputes:

  • Score disagreements
  • Line call disputes during match
  • Scheduling conflicts
  • Conduct complaints

Resolution Process:

  1. Attempt to resolve directly: Talk to opponent first
  2. Use dispute button: For score disagreements, click "Dispute" instead of confirm
  3. Contact admin: Use contact form or WhatsApp for other disputes
  4. Admin decision: Admin reviews evidence and makes final decision

Evidence:

  • Photos of scoreboards (if available)
  • WhatsApp conversation screenshots
  • Witness statements from other players
  • Video evidence (if available)

Admin Decision:

  • Admin decision is final
  • Both teams will be notified of the outcome
  • Admin may impose penalties if dispute was frivolous

Response Time:

  • Reschedule/substitute requests: Within 24 hours
